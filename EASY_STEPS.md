# EASIEST Way to Get APK (5 Minutes!)

## ‚ö° Quick Steps Using Free Online Tools

### Step 1: Upload Your Game to Netlify (FREE)
1. Go to: **https://app.netlify.com/drop**
2. Simply **drag and drop your entire "flappy bird" folder**
3. Wait 10 seconds
4. **Copy the URL** they give you (like: `https://something.netlify.app`)

### Step 2: Create APK with PWABuilder (FREE)
1. Go to: **https://www.pwabuilder.com**
2. Paste your Netlify URL in the box
3. Click **"Start"**
4. Wait for it to analyze (30 seconds)
5. Click on **"Android"** tab at top
6. Click **"Generate Package"**
7. Click **"Download"** button
8. You'll get your APK file!

### Step 3: Install on Your Phone
1. Transfer the APK file to your Android phone
2. On your phone, go to **Settings** ‚Üí **Security**
3. Enable **"Install from Unknown Sources"**
4. Open the APK file and install
5. Play your game! üéÆ
